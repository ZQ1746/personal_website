# Zhengxi Qian's Academic Website

Personal academic website for Zhengxi Qian, PhD Candidate at National University of Singapore (NUS) in Digital Financial Technology, specializing in Empirical Asset Pricing and Machine Learning.

**Website:** https://Tsien4763.github.io

## About

This is the repository for my academic portfolio website, built on the [Academic Pages template](https://github.com/academicpages/academicpages.github.io).

### Research Interests

My research agenda integrates Demand System Asset Pricing (DSAP) and high-dimensional statistical methods to revisit foundational empirical topics, including:
- Market disagreement
- Comovement  
- Liquidity
- AI and Financial Markets
- Digital Assets and Cryptocurrencies

## Directory Structure

- `_pages/` - Main pages (About, CV, Publications)
- `_publications/` - Academic publications
- `_data/` - Configuration files (navigation, authors, etc.)
- `files/` - PDF files (CV, papers, slides)
- `images/` - Images and photos
- `assets/` - CSS, JavaScript, fonts
