I am a PhD Candidate at NUS [Asian Institute of Digital Finance (AIDF)](https://www.aidf.nus.edu.sg/), specialising in Empirical Asset Pricing and Machine Learning. 

## Research Interests

My research agenda integrates Demand System Asset Pricing (DSAP) and high-dimensional statistical methods to revisit foundational empirical topics, including: 
- Market disagreement
- Comovement
- Liquidity
