## Submitted Papers

**Market Agent Nexus: Agent-Based Simulation for Financial Forecasting**  
Kelvin J.L. Koa, Yuncong Liu, **Zack C. H. Tsien**, Qi Fu, Yunshan Ma, Ke-Wei Huang  
*Submitted to International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML), 2026*

## Working Papers

**Active Dispersion: Does it Measure Differential Information or Short-selling Constraints?**  
*Work in Progress*

This paper investigates the economic interpretation of active dispersion in equity markets, examining whether it captures differential information among investors or reflects binding short-selling constraints.
